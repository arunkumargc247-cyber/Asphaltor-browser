APEX Hosts Global AI & Web3 Summit 2026

By APEX Media Desk October 12, 2026 3 Min Read

The technological landscape is shifting at an unprecedented pace, and APEX University continues to stand at the epicenter of this revolution. This past week, our main auditorium transformed into a global nexus for innovation as we proudly hosted the 2026 Global AI & Web3 Summit.

Drawing over 2,000 attendees, including students, researchers, and venture capitalists, the summit provided an exclusive platform to explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and decentralized networks. Keynote speakers featured prominent industry leaders and chief engineers from tech giants such as Google, Meta, and Tesla.

Bridging the Gap Between Theory and Industry

One of the major highlights of the summit was the interactive panel discussion on "The Ethical Framework of Generative AI in Core Engineering." The session challenged our Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering students to consider how autonomous systems will impact traditional manufacturing and data privacy.

"The engineers we are producing at APEX aren't just learning how to use tomorrow's technology; they are actively building it. This summit proves that our students are ready for the global stage."
— Dr. Arindam Sen, Dean of Computer Science

Furthermore, the university announced a new ₹5 Crore incubation fund dedicated to student-led startups focusing on Web3 infrastructure and IoT (Internet of Things) security. This initiative aligns directly with APEX's vision of fostering an entrepreneurial ecosystem on campus.

Looking Ahead

The summit concluded with a massive hackathon where 50 teams competed to build sustainable blockchain solutions for local supply chains. The winning team, representing the Data Science department, walked away with a cash prize of ₹2 Lakhs and a direct interview pipeline with a leading Silicon Valley firm.

As APEX University continues to expand its global footprint, events like the AI & Web3 Summit reinforce our commitment to providing students with unparalleled exposure to the tools and thought leaders shaping our future.
